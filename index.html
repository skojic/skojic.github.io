About Me

Hi! My name is Srdjan Kojic, self taught full stack developer with over 15 years in the industry. But I wasn’t always a developer, I was systems engineer for 10 years, doing mostly coding and design in free time, and as a hobby. Later on I discovered that developing makes me much more happy and it’s much more interesting for me than systems engineering. That’s when I completely left systems department and jumped with both feet In the Developing field. Coming from the computer background with desire to constantly evolve It wasn’t hard for me to take the learning path and try to be as best as I can be. Along the way I always put strong emphasis on the clients and projects requirements, user experience and user interaction with the website or app that I’m building. My niche is to give you complete modern simple and yet effective solutions, mixing your ideas and needs with my suggestions. People who know me personally, say that I am ego-less, humble, and creative person.
